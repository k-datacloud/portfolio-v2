kaiseidev

outline

en ja

I created a website to showcase my web development and design services.

The design was crafted using Figma, with a focus on creating a modern, simple, and refined look. I paid particular attention to softening corners in UI elements such as sections and buttons. Since the target audience includes not only Japanese users but also people from abroad, I incorporated more English text throughout the site.

For the implementation, I included interactive elements such as accordions and scroll animations. I also added language-switching functionality to allow users to view answers or content in multiple languages.
