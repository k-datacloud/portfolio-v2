君の近く

outline

en ja

I created a website to introduce a lemon farm. When starting the design, I focused on keeping it simple with white and black as the base, starting with minimal decorations and avoiding unnecessary information

I chose yellow as an accent color and used the Noto Sans JP font to convey a sense of approachability and warmth. I also paid attention to leaving ample white space to express stability and calmness in the site's visuals

For the logo, I aimed to create a simple yet cute impression.
