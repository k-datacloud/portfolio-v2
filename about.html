background

I’m a 22-year-old, born and raised in Hyogo, Japan. I taught myself programming, starting with website development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Later, I got into design, and now I enjoy creating and exploring modern and simple designs, as well as designs that focus on typography.

I’m learning more about design and front-end development, aiming to create seamless and dynamic experiences.

fun facts

I’m currently learning English by myself. At first, I started just because I wanted to try something new.

But over time, it has become a part of my life, giving me new entertainment and perspectives. It also inspires me to create designs in English, not just in Japanese.

I’m looking forward to meeting new people through English—feel free to reach out and let’s chat!
